About Us

Annasaheb Sadashiv Navale has been in the hardware business since 1994. We
have been doing sales since 1991. Our main motive initially was to supply all kinds of
agricultural equipment to the farmers in nearby rural areas and construction
materials to the contractors. We continue to do it so far. Due to our consistent efforts
of supplying the tools over a decade, we continued to grow in the business.
Currently, Satwik Annasaheb Navale is continuing the legacy of Sangam Enterprises.
We supply all kinds of construction materials and safety materials. We prefer
renowned brands like TATA, Falcon, and Samruddhi. We always believe in
upgradation as per changing market conditions. Currently we fulfill the idea of
supplying equipment in Sangamner and rural areas around.
We have a vision of wholesale hardware equipment. We are working towards it
successfully.
